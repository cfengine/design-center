."Declarative" vs. "Imperative" Programming

[quote, Mike English, 'http://spin.atomicobject.com/2012/09/13/from-imperative-to-declarative-system-configuration-with-puppet/']
______
A declarative programming style ... is often unfamiliar to newcomers, even if they are experienced programmers in other domains. Most commonly-used programming languages are examples of imperative programming, in which the programmer must describe a specific algorithm or process.  Declarative programming instead focuses on describing the particular state or goal be be achieved.
