CFEngine ships with a standard library of promise
bodies dealing with common aspects of system
administration.  

As of CFEngine 3.1.5, there are 88 promise bodies
in cfengine_stdlib.cf.

As of CFEngine 3.2.1, there are 99 promise bodies
in cfengine_stdlib.cf (and 19 bundles)

As of CFEngine 3.3.5, there are 114 promise bodies
in cfengine_stdlib.cf (and 29 bundles)

As of CFEngine 3.3.8, there are 113 promise bodies
in cfengine_stdlib.cf (and 26 bundles)
